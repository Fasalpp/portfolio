Outbreak: Dead Zone

A fast-paced zombie shooter where you defend a fortress against waves of mutants, chasing higher scores in Normal and Hard modes.

Overview

Outbreak: Dead Zone is a score-based first-person shooter set around a walled village during a zombie outbreak. The player holds the line against incoming infected, trying to achieve the highest kill count possible in each difficulty. Every run is about survival, accuracy, and beating your previous best.
