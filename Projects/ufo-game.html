Stellar Drift: UFO Escape

A fast, obstacle-dodging UFO game where you weave through laser corridors and drifting asteroids, trying to reach the end without crashing.

Overview

Stellar Drift: UFO Escape is a small arcade-style game focused on precise movement and quick reactions. You control a UFO that must fly through two dangerous zones: a tight corridor filled with laser beams and moving doors, and an open-space section packed with drifting asteroids. Touch anything, and the run is over.

Details

  • Engine: Unity
  • Language: C#
  • Role: Solo developer (design, programming, level layout)
  • Core Features: Two distinct levels (laser corridor & asteroid field), smooth UFO movement, laser beam hazards, moving doors, asteroid obstacles, and simple retry-based progression.
